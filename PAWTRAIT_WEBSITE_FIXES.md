# Pawtrait Co — Website Fix Brief
## For Claude Code via Antigravity

---

## Context

These are specific fixes to the existing Pawtrait Co website based on a full mobile UX review. Do not make any changes outside of what is listed below. Read the current state of each section before making changes.

**Important pricing note:** The print upsell credit has been updated to $39 (not $50). All references to credit amounts on the page should reflect $39 off, not $50 off. Strikethrough listed prices should be $39 higher than the "You Pay" price shown.

---

## Fix List

### 1. Hero Section — Image Composition
**Issue:** The dog and poster are being cropped awkwardly at the bottom of the hero before the CTA button appears. The full composition is not visible.
**Fix:** Adjust the hero image height or reposition the image so the full dog and poster are visible above the CTA button. The hero should feel complete, not cut off.

---

### 2. Hero Section — Remove Secondary CTA
**Issue:** "View the Gallery" secondary button is sitting directly beneath the primary "Create Your Poster — $39" button and competing with it.
**Fix:** Remove the "View the Gallery" button from the hero section entirely. One CTA only in the hero. If a gallery link is needed it can live in the navigation menu.

---

### 3. Gallery Section — Subheading Copy
**Issue:** The subheading reads "Each portrait is made from your photo — your pet's face, their personality, their story." The word "portrait" implies a traditional painted portrait which misrepresents the product.
**Fix:** Replace subheading with:
> "Each poster is built from your photo — your pet's face, their personality, their moment."

---

### 4. How It Works — Step 2 Copy
**Issue:** Step 2 currently references choosing framed or canvas print delivery, implying the print is mandatory rather than optional.
**Fix:** Replace Step 2 description with:
> "Your pet is transformed into a cinematic movie star in our signature Heist style."
Remove any mention of print selection or delivery from this step. The print upsell is handled separately after upload.

---

### 5. Upload Section — Delivery Timeframe Copy
**Issue:** "Delivered in 3 business days" is ambiguous — it could mean the digital proof or the physical print.
**Fix:** Replace with:
> "Digital proof within 3 business days"

---

### 6. Print Upsell Section — Verify and Update Credit Amount
**Issue:** The credit amount has been changed from $50 to $39. All references to the credit and all strikethrough pricing must reflect this.
**Fix:**
- Update the subheading to read: *"Your $39 digital purchase earns you a $39 credit toward any print."*

- Correct listed prices should be:

- Any text on the page referencing "$50 off" or "$50 credit" should be updated to "$39 off" or "$39 credit"

---

### 7. Payment Logos — Add PayPal
**Issue:** PayPal is missing from the payment options row beneath the "GET MY PORTRAIT" button. Afterpay and Zip are present but PayPal Pay in 4 is not shown.
**Fix:** Add PayPal logo to the payment logos row so it reads:
Afterpay | Zip | PayPal | Visa | Mastercard

---

### 8. FAQ — Replace "Paint" Language
**Issue:** The FAQ question reads "What animals do you paint?" which implies traditional painting and misrepresents the product.
**Fix:** Replace question with:
> "Which pets can you create a poster for?"
Ensure the answer is updated to match if it also references painting.

---

### 9. Footer — Social Media Icons
**Issue:** Social icons are showing as placeholder labels (IG / TK / PT) and may not be linked to real accounts.
**Fix:** Either:
- Link each icon to the real Pawtrait Co social accounts if they exist, OR
- Remove the social icons from the footer entirely until real accounts are live

Do not leave placeholder/unlinked social icons in the footer. A dead link is worse than no link.

---
